### Division
$$ f(x)=\frac{g(x)}{h(x)} \therefore f'(x) = \frac{h(x).g'(x)-g(x).h'(x)}{[h(x)]^2} $$

### Regla de la cadena
$$ f(x) = (3x^2 - 5x)^3 $$
$$ f(x) = 3(3x^2 - 5x)^2 . (6x -5) $$
$$ f(x) = (3x^2 - 5x)^2 . (18x -15) $$
